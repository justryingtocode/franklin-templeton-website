SWP Calculator
SWP is a facility available for investors to withdraw their investments systematically. The investor can select the amount and the interval of withdrawal. SWP is a great option for earning a regular income. Retirees and people with multiple dependents can use SWP as a source of regular income. SWP calculator helps in determining the final value of an investment after withdrawing a fixed amount regularly while considering the interest earned from the investment.
